# EcoTwin Intelligence

## Chosen Vertical

Sustainability & Climate Intelligence

EcoTwin Intelligence is an AI-powered Digital Carbon Twin platform designed to help users understand, track, and improve the environmental impact of their lifestyle decisions.

## Approach and Logic

The platform collects lifestyle-related inputs such as transportation habits, food consumption, electricity usage, shopping activities, waste generation, and travel patterns.

These inputs are processed through a carbon-emission modeling engine that:

* Calculates carbon footprint estimates
* Generates sustainability scores
* Forecasts future environmental impact
* Produces explainable insights
* Provides personalized recommendations

The system creates a digital representation of a user's sustainability profile, enabling data-driven environmental decision-making.

## How the Solution Works

1. User logs daily lifestyle activities.
2. The feature engineering layer transforms raw inputs into carbon-impact features.
3. Emission models calculate category-wise and total emissions.
4. Sustainability scores are generated.
5. Forecasting modules predict future trends.
6. Explainable AI modules provide transparency.
7. Recommendation engines suggest actions to reduce emissions.
8. Interactive dashboards visualize insights.

## Assumptions Made

* Emission factors are based on generalized sustainability datasets and publicly available estimates.
* User-provided lifestyle information is accurate.
* Forecasting outputs are indicative and intended for awareness rather than regulatory reporting.
* The platform is designed for educational and decision-support purposes.
